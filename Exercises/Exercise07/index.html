Assignment 2 Pitch

The Virtual Doctor

The Idea

I was very sick last week with an ear infection, laryngitis and plenty of other cold stuff. No worries, I'm no longer contagious! Through my non-stop visits to the clinic and constant questioning of what kind of sickness I was experiencing, my dad let me know that I was able to make an appointment online to see a doctor first thing in the morning, at my requested clinic. I was able to connect my cell-phone number to the online application and got constant text messages and calls to remind me of my appointment, and how many clients were before me. Sure enough, I was able go to my appointment at the time it had indicated, without waiting endless hours at the clinic to see a doctor. I thought it was a marvelous idea, certainly a huge step forward from all the other years I went to that same clinic and would wait seven hours just to get a prescription for ear infection medication.

I've always been interested in the evolution of technology and its relationship with businesses and institutions. I began to ask myself how the evolution of the technology used in medical services would develop by the year 3000. Then it came to me- what if doctors and medical services all became a virtual reality, and objects such as your computer mouse would be used to track health instead of an actual physical human being doing this for you? Would doctors become obsolete? Would medication be provided only through delivery and places like the pharmacies would no longer exist? Would this virtual reality simply become part of a world that avoids all types of human to human interactions?

And so, for my final assignment, I would like to experiment with these ideas and use the web as a means to provide this virtual reality that I am currently speculating about. I would like to use things such as the webcam, the computer mouse, and various other applications that humans use while operating the web and turn them into health tracking devices. I would also like to add a commercial side to this virtual reality, such as a calculator that tells you what kind of medication/cosmetic you should use based on your health records, and offer a link to purchase the item.

Medical Applications to be Integrated

Diagnosis calculator

Filtered search/popup of medication/cosmetic that the virtual doctor recommends + pushes you to buy

Heart rate monitor

Detection of mood through webcam

Agenda (ex knows if you're travelling, offers you a special type of sunscreen)

Workout + nutrition logger (tells you if you're in shape or not, pushed you to exercise)
